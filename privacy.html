LumiPDF Privacy

Privacy Policy

LumiPDF is designed to stay local. In the GitHub Pages demo, document content and app settings remain in your browser unless you choose to export, print, or use an external service built into the page.

What stays on your device

Draft content, theme settings, font preferences, and related app preferences are stored in local storage on your device.

What leaves your device

The site does not send your document content to a backend service. Standard browser requests may still be made for assets such as fonts or page resources.

Third-party services

The public demo may load fonts from Google Fonts. Those requests are handled by your browser and are subject to the provider's own terms and policies.

Your choices

You can clear local storage and site data through your browser settings at any time.
