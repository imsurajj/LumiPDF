LumiPDF Privacy

Privacy Policy

LumiPDF is designed to be local-first. The public web page does not collect your document content, and the app stores only the settings you choose to save in your browser or device.

Data stored on your device

LumiPDF may store draft content, theme preferences, font choices, and other app settings locally in your browser or device storage. This data stays under your control and is not used to identify you.

Data we do not collect

The LumiPDF web page does not require sign-in, does not ask for account creation, and does not intentionally send your document content to a backend service. The page is built to work without uploading your files.

Third-party services

The public demo may load fonts or other standard web assets from trusted third-party services. Those requests are made by your browser and are governed by the provider's own policies.

Your controls

You can clear local storage, browser cache, and site data through your browser settings at any time. If you do not want a setting saved, simply do not enable or store it.

Retention

Data stored locally remains on your device until you remove it. LumiPDF does not maintain a cloud copy of your documents from this web page.

Policy summary

  • No account is required to use the public page.
  • Document content stays local unless you choose to export or share it yourself.
  • Settings are stored only when you save them in the browser or device.
  • Third-party requests are limited to normal browser-loaded assets such as fonts.
